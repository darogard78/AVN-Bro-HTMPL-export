Overview: The king is dead leaving a power vacuum in the kingdom. Help guide the Princess on her quest to get herself upon the throne. You'll have to manage her classes and personality as she tries to gain support. But be careful, dangers are lurking everywhere and the Princess has a mind of her own. Naked Ambition features a pixel art style inspired by mid 90's PC games as well as a unique semi-randomized gameplay style. What order events happen in and what decisions the Princess makes changes every game.​
