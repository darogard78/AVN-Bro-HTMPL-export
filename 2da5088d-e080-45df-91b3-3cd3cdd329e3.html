Overview: Angela (aka Angel) grew up in the Midwest and has always been a good girl. She went to church every Sunday, studied hard in school, and was shy around boys. On Angel's 18th birthday, she decides to move to L.A. with her Dad and brothers. Angel is about to begin her final year of High school and is enrolled in a private school. She isn't sure if she will make it into a decent college because she doesn't make good enough grades to get a scholarship and doesn't have the money for tuition. A very different life awaits her in L.A. and the adventure begins very quickly for the naive Midwestern girl. Make the choices that shape her as a Pure, Naive, or Experimental girl as she makes her way through each day in the big city with the unexpected experiences awaiting her.
