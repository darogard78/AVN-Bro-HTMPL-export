Following the fallout of the megacorporation's war, the government finally ceded...Incapable of holding back the rampant corruption and violence that now flowed out onto the streets. What followed was an era of breakneck economic progress and development we could hardly keep up with. Cities quickly became independent megacities with their own laws, cultures and underground businesses. In the aftermath, the many sciences prospered. Artificial Intelligence, Virtual reality applications, gene editing, food printing to feed the people and development of advanced robotics all led to a higher standard of life...For a price of course. Now you have become a target, and they'll stop at nothing to take what's yours. ...But you're not going down without a fight.
